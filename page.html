What Course Calendar Can Do

Course Calendar Can:

  • Place your courses onto your Bmail Google Calendar.
  • Account for academic calendar days off and breaks.
  • Account for academic calendar early dismissals like Passover.
  • Account for all academic calendar "Monday classes meet not Tuesday classes" days.
  • Place events from the in person academic calendar or online academic calendar on to your Bmail Google Calendar.
  • Update class information on your calendar if you come back and use it again ONLY IF there is an official change in the system. Ex. The course room has changed in the system.

What Course Calendar CANNOT Do

Course Calendar CANNOT do a lot of things. Read below to ensure you understand how it works.

  • Course Calendar CANNOT automatically delete dropped courses from your Google Calendar. You must do that yourself.
  • Course Calendar CANNOT automatically add new courses to your Google Calendar. You must come back and use it again to add any new courses.
  • Course Calendar is only accurate up to the point you used it, the given academic calendar, and the official course schedule.
    • Course Calendar CANNOT update your Google Calendar for class cancellations like snow days, or any other reason.
    • Course Calendar CANNOT update your Google Calendar for any professor change or late change to the academic calendar.
  • Course Calendar CANNOT add asynchronous classes to your Google Calendar.
  • Course Calendar CANNOT be used as an excuse to not attend class.

Instructions

  1. Select your options using the "Select Options" button below. The selected semester and courses for that semester will be displayed.
  2. Ensure the displayed courses are the ones you intend to add to your Google Calendar.
  3. Add your courses to your Google Calendar by pressing the "Post to Google Calendar" button.
  4. Enjoy the convenience of not having to do all this Google Calendar work yourself every single semester.
